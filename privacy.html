Privacy Policy

Your data stays yours.

Last updated: March 2026

The short version: AI Watcher collects nothing. Your session credentials are stored entirely on your own devices within Apple's secure App Group container. No data is ever sent to our servers — because there are no servers.

Information We Collect

AI Watcher does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. The only data the app handles are the session credentials (cookies) required to query the Claude API on your behalf. These are:

  • Your Claude sessionKey cookie — used to authenticate API requests
  • Your Claude lastActiveOrg cookie — used to identify your organization

These credentials are captured automatically when you sign in through the in-app browser and are written only to an Apple App Group shared container on your device.

How Your Data Is Used

Your credentials are used for one purpose only: making authenticated requests to the Claude API to retrieve your current usage statistics. These requests are made directly from your device to Anthropic's servers — AI Watcher is never in the middle.

Usage data (your percentage consumed, time to reset) is displayed within the app, on your Apple Watch, and in your home screen widgets. It is never logged, analyzed, or stored beyond what is displayed on screen.

Data Storage & Security

All credentials are stored in an Apple App Group container — a secure, sandboxed storage mechanism shared only between AI Watcher's own app targets (iPhone app, Apple Watch app, and widget extension). This storage is:

  • Isolated from all other apps on your device
  • Protected by iOS's built-in data protection
  • Never backed up to iCloud or any external service
  • Deleted when you uninstall AI Watcher

Cross-Device Sync

When syncing credentials from your iPhone to your Apple Watch, AI Watcher uses Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This communication travels over the encrypted, device-to-device channel Apple provides — it does not pass through any external server or network endpoint controlled by AI Watcher.

Third-Party Services

AI Watcher interacts with one external service: the Anthropic Claude API. When the app fetches your usage data, your device sends a request directly to Anthropic's infrastructure using your session credentials. This interaction is governed by Anthropic's own privacy policy and terms of service, not ours.

AI Watcher has no affiliation with Anthropic and is not endorsed by Anthropic.

Analytics & Tracking

AI Watcher contains no analytics SDKs, no crash reporting services, no advertising frameworks, and no third-party tracking of any kind. There is no account system, no sign-up, and no way for us to identify individual users.

Children's Privacy

AI Watcher is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app requires an active Claude subscription, which itself requires the user to be of appropriate age per Anthropic's terms.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of AI Watcher after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
